Eat to support your training

You don’t need a perfect diet. Focus on simple rules you can follow most of the time.

The simple plate method

  • ½ plate: vegetables and fruit.
  • ¼ plate: protein (chicken, fish, tofu, beans, eggs).
  • ¼ plate: carbs (rice, potatoes, pasta, whole grains).
  • Add a little healthy fat (olive oil, nuts, avocado).

To gain weight, increase portions slightly. To lose, shrink portions a bit.

Protein basics

  • Helps you recover, stay full, and build/keep muscle.
  • Try to include protein in every meal.
  • Easy ideas: Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, tuna, beans, protein shakes.

Habits that matter more than “perfect foods”

  • Drink mostly water or low-calorie drinks.
  • Eat slowly and stop when you’re comfortably full.
  • Plan treats instead of “accidentally” snacking all day.
  • Sleep and stress management affect hunger and cravings.

This information is general and not a replacement for personalized advice.
