Build your own workout plan

Use this page to sketch a simple weekly routine. Then plug in exercises that fit your goal and equipment.

Quick weekly template

This creates a template. You can change any day or exercise.

Step 1 – Choose movement patterns

Each week, try to hit:

  • Push (push-ups, bench press, shoulder press)
  • Pull (rows, pull-ups, pulldowns)
  • Legs (squats, deadlifts, lunges, step-ups)
  • Core (planks, dead bugs, hollow holds)

Step 2 – Sets, reps, and rest

  • Strength: 3–5 sets of 3–6 reps, longer rest.
  • Muscle: 3–4 sets of 6–12 reps, medium rest.
  • Endurance: 2–3 sets of 12–20 reps, short rest.

Use a weight where you have 1–3 “good reps” left at the end of each set.

Step 3 – Exercise ideas by goal

  • Strength: squats, deadlifts, bench press, overhead press.
  • Muscle: dumbbell presses, rows, leg presses, curls, raises.
  • Fat loss: full-body circuits, brisk walking, intervals.
  • Endurance: walking, jogging, cycling, rowing.
